Got questions?

Frequently asked

Is SummarAI really free to use?
+
Yes — creating an account and generating summaries is completely free. There's no credit card required, no trial period, and no hidden charges to get started.
How accurate are the summaries?
+
SummarAI uses state-of-the-art language models via LangChain to ensure summaries are contextually accurate. The AI preserves logical flow and key arguments rather than just extracting keywords. We target a 98% key-point retention rate across academic content.
What types of content work best?
+
Currently, SummarAI supports plain text input — perfect for lecture notes, articles, chapters, and research excerpts. PDF and image support are planned for future releases. Any written content works well regardless of subject matter.
Is there a text length limit?
+
SummarAI handles content of varying lengths, from a single paragraph to several pages. Very long inputs are processed using smart chunking techniques to ensure every section is covered accurately.
Can I access my past summaries?
+
Yes. Every summary you generate is stored in your personal dashboard, organized by date. You can review, copy, or delete them at any time from your account.
Will PDF and image support be added?
+
Yes — this is actively in development. The roadmap includes uploading PDFs and images directly, with the AI extracting and summarizing the content automatically.
